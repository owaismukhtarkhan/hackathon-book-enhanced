# Physical AI Principles

## Introduction to Physical AI

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional digital AI to **Embodied Intelligence**â€”AI systems that understand and interact with the physical world. Unlike conventional AI that operates purely in digital environments, Physical AI systems must comprehend physics, perception, motion, and real-world interaction.

### The Digital Brain vs. Physical Body

The core challenge in Physical AI is bridging the gap between:

- The **digital brain** (AI models, planning, perception)
- The **physical body** (robots, sensors, actuators)

This integration requires AI systems to understand:
- Physical laws and constraints
- Sensor data from the real world
- Actuator responses and environmental feedback
- Safety and reliability in physical interactions

### Key Concepts in Physical AI

1. **Embodied Cognition**: Intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its environment
2. **Sensorimotor Integration**: Seamless coordination between sensing and acting
3. **Physical Reasoning**: Understanding how objects behave in 3D space
4. **Real-time Processing**: Making decisions under physical and temporal constraints
